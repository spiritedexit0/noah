The Sacred Recipe

The recipe was found during the great archeological discovery period in Germany in 1891. The notebook the recipe
was in was determined to be more than 500 years old through advanced carbon-dating methods. The most famous theory
as to how the notebook ended up in the past suggest that during his travels, Noah accidentally left this notebook in the past
and wasn't aware of this until much later. The existance of the notebook also gives rise to the famous causality paradox.
